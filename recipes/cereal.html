Fruit Loops

Description

Have you ever woken up feeling hungry? What about waking up thirsty too? Euch, it's the worst, isn't it? Well, never fear, as I have the perfect recipe to kick start your day that'll solve both these problems! It's called cereal, and it couldn't be easier.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal

Steps

  1. Get your milk from the fridge
  2. Get your cereal from the cupboard
  3. Pour a generous helping of your cereal into a nice big bowl (go on, eat a few while they're dry too, teehee
  4. Splash a tsunami of delicious milky white goodness over your heap of cereal
  5. Grab a spoon and dig in champ, it's time to start the day!
