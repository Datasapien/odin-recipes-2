Peppermint Tea

Description

Spent the day asking kids to listen? To stop chewing their pens? To stop hitting each other? To sit down? To be quiet? Well, sounds like you need a cup of peppermint tea, my good friend.

Ingredients

  • Peppermint tea
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil the kettle
  2. Put teabag in a cup
  3. Pour freshly boiled water into the cup
  4. Enjoy the sensation of being teleported to a peaceful Buddhist monastary - no teens here!
