Wish Sandwich

Description

We've all been there - you open the cupboard and all you see is some crusty bread and nothing to go with it. Don't you wish you had some meat to go with your sandwich? Well, here's what to do!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Positive Attitude

Steps

  1. Get your crusty bread out of the cupboard
  2. Put the slices on a plate
  3. Close your eyes and imagine all the tasty fillings your heart desires
  4. Take a big bite, and try not to choke on the dry crumbs
